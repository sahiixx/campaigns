# Ghost Systems — Loom Demo Script (5 min)

## Setup
- Screen: Vapi dashboard + Ghost backend dashboard side-by-side
- Mic: Clear, no background noise
- Start with camera on for 10s, then screen only

---

## Script

**[0:00] Hook — Camera on**
"Hey, I'm Sahil. I built something for HVAC companies that's recovering $10,000+ a month in lost leads. Let me show you exactly how it works in 5 minutes."

**[0:20] The Problem — Screen: Google search "HVAC near me"**
"I just searched 'HVAC near me' in Phoenix. Look at these results — every single one of these companies has a phone number. Now let me call three of them after 6 PM..."

**[0:45] Voicemail demo — Screen: phone dialer (simulated)**
"Company one — voicemail. Company two — voicemail. Company three — voicemail. It's 7:30 PM, someone's AC just died in 105-degree heat, and they're going straight to competitor #4 who answers."

**[1:10] Introduce Ghost — Screen: Vapi assistant config**
"This is Ghost Systems. It's an AI receptionist named Aria that answers your calls 24/7, books appointments into your calendar, and texts your technician instantly. Let me show you a live test call."

**[1:40] Live demo — Screen: test_call.py output or actual Vapi call**
"I'm calling right now. Listen..."
[Play test call or read simulation]
"Aria just qualified the lead — residential, cooling emergency, same-day. She checked availability, booked 2 PM, and sent a text summary to the tech. All in 90 seconds."

**[2:30] Backend proof — Screen: Ghost dashboard at localhost:8000**
"Here's the backend dashboard. Every call, every booking, every transcript — logged in real time. You can see exactly how many calls Aria handled, how many she booked, and your revenue recovery."

**[3:00] ROI math — Screen: simple calculator or slide**
"Let's do the math. One emergency HVAC job averages $800. If you're missing just 2 after-hours calls per week, that's $6,400/month in lost revenue. Ghost costs $1,200/month. That's a 5.3x return — and that's conservative. Most clients see 8-12x."

**[3:40] Social proof — Screen: quote_generator example**
"Here's a real quote we sent to a Phoenix company last week. They had pain score 3 — no website, no after-hours answering. We offered 50% off setup because the need was urgent. They signed within 24 hours."

**[4:00] Guarantee — Screen: text overlay**
"Here's the guarantee: if Aria doesn't book at least 3 additional jobs in your first 30 days, month 2 is free. No questions asked. I'm that confident."

**[4:20] CTA — Camera back on**
"Two ways to move forward: One, reply to my email with 'DEMO' and I'll send you this exact 5-minute video customized for your company. Two, book a 15-minute call at calendly.com/sahil and we'll have you live within 48 hours."

**[4:50] Close**
"Ghost Systems — never miss another emergency call. Talk soon."

**Client:** _________________________  
**Date:** _________________________

## 1. SERVICES
ReviewReply will:
- Monitor connected Google Business Profiles for new reviews
- Auto-draft AI responses matching client's brand voice
- Allow approval/editing before posting
- Post approved responses automatically
- Provide monthly analytics: response rate, sentiment trends, top keywords

## 2. LIMITATIONS
- Supports Google Business Profile reviews only (not Yelp, Facebook, etc. unless Agency plan)
- AI responses require human approval on Starter plan
- ReviewReply is not liable for responses posted after client approval

## 3. DATA & PRIVACY
- Client retains ownership of all review data
- ReviewReply does not store customer PII beyond review text and rating
- Data deleted within 30 days of account cancellation

## 4. PAYMENT
- Monthly fee charged on 1st of each month
- Prorated refunds for annual prepay only
- Failed payment: 3-day grace period, then account pause

## 5. TERMINATION
Either party may cancel with 7 days notice before next billing cycle.

## Description

### The Problem
You're juggling 4+ clients, tracking hours in notes apps, invoices in email threads, and payment status in your head. Every month ends with the same panic: *"Who still owes me money? Did I already bill this?"*

Freelancers lose 10-15% of their income to poor tracking — not because they don't work hard, but because they don't have a system.

### The Solution
This Notion dashboard gives you a complete freelance command center in under 10 minutes:

- **Client Database** — Every client, contact info, rate, and project history in one table
- **Project Tracker** — Active projects, deadlines, deliverables, and status at a glance
- **Invoice Generator** — Auto-calculate totals from tracked hours. One click to see what's billable.
- **Income Dashboard** — Monthly revenue, outstanding payments, and year-to-date earnings
- **Tax-Ready Exports** — Clean summaries for your accountant. No more spreadsheet archaeology.

### What You Get
- 1 complete Notion template (duplicate to your workspace instantly)
- 3 pre-built views: Client List, Project Pipeline, Income Overview
- 10+ linked databases that talk to each other
- Formula fields that auto-calculate rates, totals, and overdue status
- Step-by-step setup guide (5 minutes to configure)
- Lifetime updates — new features added free

### Who This Is For
- Freelance writers, designers, developers, marketers, VA's
- Anyone billing by project or hourly
- Solo operators who hate spreadsheets
- People who want to look professional without learning new software

### Who This Is NOT For
- Agencies with 10+ employees (you need a real CRM)
- People who love Excel and won't switch
- Anyone expecting an automated time tracker (this is manual input, but fast)
